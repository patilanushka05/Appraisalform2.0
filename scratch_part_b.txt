GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS
This appraisal form covers the performance period of Academic Year 2026–27 and must be filled by every full-time faculty member.
The form has four parts — Part A (Teaching & Learning), Part B (Research & Innovation), Part C (Administrative Role & University Development Contribution) and Part D (Annual Confidential Report, completed by the School Director only).
Faculty must enter figures in the 'Faculty' score column only, for every row they add. The 'HOD' and 'Director' columns are for evaluator use only and must not be filled by faculty.
All claims must be supported by documentary evidence as listed in the 'Evidence Required' column. Undocumented claims are liable to be scored zero by the evaluator.
Where DYPIU affiliation is a mandatory requirement (e.g., journal publications), claims without proof of DYPIU affiliation will not be considered.
Marks for items with multiple authors/contributors must be apportioned as per the split ratios specified against each parameter.
Attach a numbered list of enclosures corresponding to each claim at the end of the form.
Any additional achievement not explicitly covered may be recorded under 'Any Other Contribution' in the relevant part, subject to evaluator approval.
This form must be submitted through the faculty's Head of Department, followed by verification at School Director and respective Dean levels as detailed in the Verification & Approval section.
